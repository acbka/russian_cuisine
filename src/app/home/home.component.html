How can meals be stored, and for how long?

We deliver fresh dishes, and the packaging and storage of meals we use allows the dishes to remain fresh and tasty in the refrigerator for up to a week.

You should follow simple rules so that the dishes do not deteriorate prematurely:

  • Use only dry and clean appliances
  • Close containers tightly
  • Store containers in the refrigerator (between +3 - +5 degrees)
  • Reheat meals in portions as needed.
